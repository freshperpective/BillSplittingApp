# Privacy Policy — Sorted

*Last updated: June 7, 2026*

## What data we collect

Sorted collects only your **email address**, which you provide when you sign in.

## Why we collect it

Your email address is used solely to identify your account and allow you to sign in. We do not use it for marketing, and we do not share it with third parties.

## Where it is stored

Your data — your email address and the group expense records you create — is stored securely with [Supabase](https://supabase.com), our backend provider. Supabase hosts data on infrastructure in the United States. All data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.2+) and at rest.

## What we do not collect

- Location data
- Device identifiers or advertising IDs
- Contacts or address book
- Health or financial account data
- Usage analytics or crash reports beyond server-level Supabase logs

## Third-party services

Sorted uses **Supabase** for authentication and data storage. No advertising networks, analytics SDKs, or other third-party tracking services are included in the app.

Supabase's privacy policy: [supabase.com/privacy](https://supabase.com/privacy)

## Data deletion

To delete your account and all associated data, email us at reviewerwriter424@gmail.com. We will delete your data within 30 days of your request.

## Changes to this policy

If we change what data we collect or how we use it, we will update this page and bump the "Last updated" date above.
