## Full description (paste into Play Console)

Sorted makes splitting group expenses effortless.

Whether it's a holiday with friends, a flat share, a dinner out, or a work trip — add an expense in seconds and Sorted tracks exactly who owes what.

**SPLIT MODES**
• Equal — divide evenly across everyone
• Unequal — enter exact amounts per person
• Percent — split by percentage
• Shares — split by ratio (e.g. 2:1:1)
• Adjust — start equal, then nudge individual amounts for tips or rounding

**MULTI-CURRENCY**
Add expenses in any currency. Sorted captures the exchange rate at entry time so your group balance always converts correctly, even on international trips.

**SETTLE UP**
Record a payment in one tap. Balances update immediately. Use "Simplify debts" to find the minimum number of transfers needed to square everyone off.

**RECEIPTS**
Attach up to 5 photos per expense — stored privately in the cloud, visible only to group members.

**ACTIVITY FEED**
A complete history of every expense added, edited, or deleted, plus every settlement and membership change — per group and across all your groups.

**THEMES**
Choose Light, Grey, Dark, or follow your system setting. All colours adapt automatically.

**HONEST AND PRIVATE**
No ads. No premium tier. No contact-list access. Your email address is the only personal data collected — used only to sign you in, never sold or shared.
